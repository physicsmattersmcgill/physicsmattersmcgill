McPherson public lecture series, which brings distinguished physicists to McGill to give both technical and public lectures, as well as public astro nights.

Despite COVID-19 restrictions, our public talks go on! Since May 2020, we have moved our public lectures to an online format. These talks are streamed on both Zoom and on Facebook Live. They’re also highly interactive - the second half of the talk is for audience questions!

To hear about our lectures, follow us on Facebook or email us at outreach@physics.mcgill.ca to be added to our mailing list!

For more online activities and resources on Science, visit the Science Outreach page of the Faculty of Science!
